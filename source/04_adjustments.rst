Adjustments and Assumptions
===========================

Adjustment Framework
--------------------

Apply consistent adjustments against each comp where relevant:

- Size delta (per-square-foot basis)
- Condition/renovation differences
- Parking/storage differences
- Floor/view/exposure differences
- Fee inclusion differences
